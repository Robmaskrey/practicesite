About Rob

I've always been interested in computers, and I've always been able to use them well enough to be the guy who fixes them for friends and family, most of the time.

I've spent my life roming outside in the woods or wasting time on the internet. I've built houses and made old woodwork look new again. I was briefly a craftsman with a high end design company here in Boston, but I didn't see a future doing that.

I chased a dream and learned PHP with some friends of mine who build their own webistes and applications. It was idyllic, and I loved our office space. I couldn't dedicate the time needed to excel, however, and I grew frustrated at my own pace. I still needed to pay bills, after all, and I couldn't justify not working at all while I developed a new skill.

While I have a long way to go, I feel confident that my ability to build on the foundation I've already laid is strong. I have a varied skill set to draw upon, and I have a lot of experiences to influence my work, and hopefully, make my final products unique and well rounded.
