Beginning to learn Python in Boston

June 1, 2018 by Rob

This website is supposed to demonstrate that I understand the basics of CSS and HTML, while I am admittedly rusty, it's starting to come back to me.

Our first day of class begings this monday, June 4th. I feel like I am behind on the pre work that was assigned to us, I underestimated how much time and energy moving would take, which left me with less time than anticipated to complete the assignments. Thankfully I had given myself experience enough with programming: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap is blessedly available, which of course makes styling websites a breeze. Hopefully we'll be covering some of that.

In the future I plan on staying farther ahead of my studies. I don't foresee this being an issue, as I have removed all other distractions and obligations. I am paying for this class, and I intend to make the most of it. My goals for this class are as follows

  • Learn to develop in python in a structured classroom setting with.
  • Dedicate not just class time, but all time over the next ten weeks to expand and improve my github portfolio.
  • Integrate what I learn with what I already know, and expand upon both.

The exact wording of these are subject to change, but the spirit at the core remains to use this time well, not squander the opportunity to ask questions, learn well and position myself well to gain employment as a programmer in the Boston area.
